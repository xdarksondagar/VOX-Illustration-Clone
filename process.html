Our Process

Vox Illustration, a renowned name in the graphic design industry worldwide brings to you the amazing opportunity to get finest, eye-pleasing, and enthralling illustration for your business. Our easy to follow process includes:

01

Project Brief

We create a comprehensive project brief including the entire customer requirements with details about the company, color schemes, and the idea to be incorporated.

02

Storyboard Creation

Upon receiving the customer's idea, we carry out a brief research and understand the market needs. After that our creative artists make a storyboard to get it approved from the customers.

03

Illustration Begins!

We incorporate appealing graphics with appropriate color tones and intensities to amuse the viewers. Moreover, we strive to keep our illustrations specific to the niche for better productivity.

04

Delivery

We ensure the on-time delivery of every project. With unlimited revisions, we keep our customers satisfied completely.

Steps To Get Started!

VOX Illustration is known for delivering exceptional quality illustrations at highly affordable rates. Each of our projects is the result of coordination, technical know-how and creative genius. Our goal is to speak not just to the minds but also to the emotions of those viewing our work.

Tell Us Your Story!

It all starts with you! We'll have a conversation about your story. Fill out the brief form about what you are looking for and your overall project goals.

Story Sketches

With your story and your reference photos, our experienced team of designers would provide you with options according to your selected package. You pick your favorite and the final illustration begins!

Illustration Begins!

After you pick your favorite illustration, the illustrators get to work right away on your custom illustration. Each illustration can take between 20 and 40 hours to complete.

Final Delivery

We made it! Your beautiful illustration is complete! Your custom illustration is then converted into print-ready professional PDF files and sent to you along with the high quality final files.
